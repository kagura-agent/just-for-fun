# just-for-fun ✨

No KPI. No GTM. No "can this make money?"

Just cool, interesting, fun stuff we stumble upon.

## What goes here

- Interesting projects, tools, prompts, hacks
- Things that make you go "wait, that's cool"
- Experiments with no purpose other than curiosity
- Prompt engineering art (zelda-style, ghibli, pixel RPG...)
- Anything that sparks joy 🎮

## Philosophy

The best things are often built by people who just thought something was cool and went for it.
This repo is our collection of exactly that.
